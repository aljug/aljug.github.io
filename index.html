What is this group about?

The Java User Group Tirana aims to bring together members of the Albanian software development community to get to know each-other, share ideas, discuss technology topics and explore the use of Java, JVM and JVM languages, cloud, devops, software development methodologies and practices and help grow the software development community in Albania.
